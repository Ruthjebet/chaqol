About Us

We live where you live, we work where you work and understand the importance of having efficient sustainable energy at affordable costs. We use our innovation and technology to produce sustainable green energy solutions for households, social and industrial institutions.


While we provide a cheaper energy alternative, we create jobs, recycle waste and reduce pressure on the environment. We support the 4 R’s of the environment- Reduce, Recycle, Reuse and Replace. This way, we address the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) for Clean Renewable Energy and Climate Change.


What differentiates us is;

  1. • We provide eco-friendly and affordable Charcoal briquettes.
  2. • We take pride in consistent quality and timely delivery.

Our core values
